Restore and save the Valley. Disney Dreamlight valley is a sim adventure game filled with Disney and Pixar characters. The valley was overturned by night thorns and The forgetting. It's your duty to restore the valley and bring everyone back. Find memories, visit realms of your favorite characters, unlock new biomes, shop, decorate, and gather resources.

Level up your friendship with everyone in the valley to unlock new quests, earn money, and get special items catered to each character. Hang out together while fishing, mining, farming, and more to level up. With each level your Disney-Pixar friend with help you get more items as you go!

Farming is one of many ways to gather resources, hangout with friends, and make money. Different seeds come with each new biome you unlock. Use your newly farmed crops to make special recipes, give to friends, or sell for profit. The star coins you earn can be used to buy daily rotating items at Scrooge McDuck's store or any of Goofy's stalls!

Scrooge McDuck's store is the valley's very own shop. Everyday he will carry new clothing and furniture items, especially the recurring blue lamp. Use your star coins to buy new clothing items to wear or furniture items to decorate either in your house or any where outside within Dreamlight Valley! All of the furniture and clothing items are themed after your favorite Disney-Pixar movies!
